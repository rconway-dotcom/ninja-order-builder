How access works
No credentials to manage — access is tied to your Shopify staff account.
1
Click Sign in with Shopify. A tab opens to your store's login.
2
Log in with your Ninja Transfers staff account. The tab closes automatically.
3
You're in. Sessions last 8 hours — you'll be asked to sign in again the next day.
4
Every draft order you build is stamped with your name and email as a Shopify metafield — full audit trail, zero extra work.
5
To revoke someone's access, just remove their staff account in Shopify Admin. No extension changes needed.
